Noisy New York

Plan your next NYC trip around 2016 Noise Complaints

New York City continues to be one of the most popular travel destinations for tourists from all over the world. Known as The City That Never Sleeps, it’s no surprise that New York is constantly bustling with activity, both day and night.

The city is so boisterous, in fact, that in 2016 NYC’s 311 received more than 200,00 noise complaints ranging from barking dogs to loud party music. And while some New Yorkers might find the city’s cacophony of sounds to be a nuisance, our team has a more positive outlook on the noise.
